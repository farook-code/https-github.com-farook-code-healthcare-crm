# Client Presentation Script: Healthcare CRM System

**Objective:** Demonstrate the complete patient journey from intake to billing in under 5 minutes.
**Presenter Role:** Super Admin (Access to all modules).

---

## 1. Introduction: The Command Center (Dashboard)
**Context:** "First, let's look at the Executive Dashboard. This gives hospital administrators a real-time pulse of the facility."

*   **Action:** Start on the **Dashboard** page.
*   **Highlight:**
    *   **Top Cards:** Point out "Total Patients" and "Today's Appointments" (Real-time operational metrics).
    *   **Financial Chart:** Show the "Income Analysis" graph (Demonstrates high-level business intelligence).
    *   **Latest Activity:** Scroll down to show that every action in the system is tracked.

## 2. Step 1: Patient Intake (Reception)
**Context:** "Let's simulate a new patient walking in. Our intake process is streamlined to reduce wait times."

*   **Action:** Click **Patients** -> **Add New Patient**.
*   **Demo Steps:**
    1.  Enter a Name (e.g., "James Bond").
    2.  Fill minimal required fields (Phone, Gender).
    3.  Click **Save**.
*   **Talking Point:** "As you can see, we generate a unique `Patient Code` instantly. This digital record will now track their entire medical history across our facility."

## 3. Step 2: Scheduling Care (Appointments)
**Context:** "Now, let's get James in to see a specialist."

*   **Action:** Go to the newly created Patient's Profile (or click **Appointments** -> **Add Appointment**).
*   **Demo Steps:**
    1.  Select **Doctor**: Choose a specialist (e.g., Dr. House - Cardiology).
    2.  Select **Date/Time**: Pick "Today".
    3.  Click **Confirm Booking**.
*   **Talking Point:** "The system automatically checks the doctor's availability to prevent double-booking. The doctor is instantly notified."

## 4. Step 3: Clinical Management (Doctor's View)
**Context:** "The doctor opens their specific dashboard to manage the consultation."

*   **Action:** Navigate to the **Appointment List** or the specific **Appointment Details** page.
*   **Highlight:**
    *   **Patient History:** Show how the doctor can see previous visits and vitals on the same screen.
*   **Action:** Click **"Add Vitals"** or **"Add Notes"**.
    *   Enter Blood Pressure: `120/80`
    *   Enter Diagnosis: `Mild Hypertension`
    *   Click **Save**.
*   **Talking Point:** "Doctors can input clinical data rapidly. This data immediately feeds into the patient's longitudinal health record."

## 5. Step 4: Billing & Revenue (Finance)
**Context:** "Finally, as the patient leaves, the billing is automated."

*   **Action:** Click **Invoices** -> **Create Invoice**.
*   **Demo Steps:**
    1.  Select the **Patient** and **Appointment**.
    2.  The system should auto-populate the **Consultation Fee** based on the doctor's profile.
    3.  **Optional:** Add a "Medicine" (Paracetamol) to show itemized billing.
    4.  Click **Generate Invoice**.
*   **Talking Point:** "The invoice combines consultation fees and pharmacy items into a single bill. We can print this or email it directly to the patient."

---

## 6. Conclusion
**Context:** "This system unifies Reception, Clinical Care, and Finance into one seamless platform, reducing administrative overhead and improving patient outcomes."
